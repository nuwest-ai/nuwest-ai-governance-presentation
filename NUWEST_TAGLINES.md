## Usage Guidelines

**"Facts, not fear."**
- Use throughout presentations when contrasting nuwest.ai approach with vendor scare tactics
- Especially effective when discussing cost projections, threat inflation, or "AI-powered" marketing
- Pairs well with dry, understated tone

**"Measure what matters."**
- Use at presentation closings
- Use in CTAs and next-steps slides
- Emphasizes actionable outcomes over process

**"Measure, don't market."** (Reserved)
- Future use for direct competitive positioning
- Anti-vendor messaging
- Could work for blog posts, thought leadership

**"Test it. Trust it. Track it."** (Reserved)
- Future use for process-oriented content
- Good for technical audiences
- Could work for product messaging (bsdetector?)
