## Technical Features

### Slide Transitions
- Main: Slide transition
- Background: Zoom transition
- **Unpredictable element:** 30% of slides randomly vary transition style
- Speed: Default (can be adjusted in index.html)

### Typography & Legibility
- Base font size: 32px
- Headers: 2.4em (h1), 1.8em (h2), 1.3em (h3)
- High contrast throughout
- Responsive design for different screen sizes

### Special Elements
- Quote boxes (yellow border on black)
- Question boxes (yellow background, large text)
- Stat displays (5em yellow numbers)
- Citation formatting (small, italic, gray)
- Tagline positioning (right-aligned, uppercase)
- Speaker notes on every slide

### Keyboard Controls
- **S:** Speaker notes (critical for practice/delivery)
- **F:** Fullscreen
- **B:** Blackout
- **ESC:** Overview mode
- **Arrow keys/Space:** Navigate
